ALL ABOUT TRIANGLES

A triangle tracker tracks your input and tells you the type of triangle your input represents
    NOTE:a triangle is formed when the sum of two dimensions are greater than the third ie (A+B) > C.
  1. An isosceles triangle is formed when two of your dimensions are similar
  2. An equilateral triangle is formed when all 3 dimensions are similar
  3. A scalene triangle is formed when all your dimensions are not equal
